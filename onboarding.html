1 Your eBay developer keys

Bynari uses these to talk to eBay as you — you bring your own keys. Nothing leaves your computer.

How do I get these?
  1. Create a free developer account at developer.ebay.com.
  2. Under Application Keys, make a Production keyset — that gives you the App ID and Cert ID.
  3. Add an OAuth redirect URI (RuName) to that keyset.
  4. Run the user consent flow once to mint your refresh token (grant the Sell/Inventory scopes).
Not sure, or stuck on the OAuth step? We help. That's what we're here for — reach out and we'll walk you through it.

2 Test the connection

Confirms your keys work and reads your business policies. Read-only — nothing is listed.

3 Pick your default policies

eBay requires a shipping, payment, and return policy plus a location on every listing. Choose your defaults — you can override per item later.

You're connected. Bynari can now build and publish listings to your eBay account — on your say-so, never automatically.
